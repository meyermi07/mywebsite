July 1st, 2024

Today, I formally launched my website with a blog post page and some much better formatting.  As I stated on my homepage, this is really just a hobby site right now, so if you are here - wonderful!  I don't anticipate I'll be able to post frequently, but as I become more familiar with the site builder tool I'm using, perhaps I can dedicate more time to the content of this site.  For now though, I'm just enjoying the fact that I even have this site up and running!

As for this blog, I really don't have too many expectations for it, but at the very least, I'd like to house my own thoughts and ideas here, if for nothing else, just to give myself an outlet to share some of the things I'm thinking about. If nothing else - I think I might just try to write occasionally to save a space for my girls to learn a little about their Dad - they are still very young, so I hope that this site can stand for some time to serve as a sort of journal entry.

It's much too late - I think I'll finish this post here.  I'll end it on a note about what I'm listening to right now... hope you enjoy too!
